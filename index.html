About Inferno

INFERNO is the flagship 3-days management fest of 'Army Institute of Management, Kolkata' which is one of the top rated private institutions under MAKAUT (formerly WBUT) located at the heart of Kolkata. This event is going to be conducted from 15th November, 2019 to 17th November, 2019. ​ INFERNO'19' will be an extravagant, one of its kind event that the capital city has seen. Over 20+ colleges will be invited to the fest with an expected footfall of over 2500 students. ​ INFERNO'19 will have events in various categories including Human Resource Management, Marketing, Business Plan, Entrepreneurship,etc. We will also have cultural events which will include dancing,singing, art and many others to encourage participation and provide platform for students to bring their talent to best use.
